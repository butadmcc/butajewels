About Us

Buta Jewelry Established in 1998 from Baku – Azerbaijan is driven by the vision to be at the forefront of technology and innovation in fashion Jewelry and delivering superior customer service.

Buta Elegant design and superb quality are the hallmarks of a Buta Jewels Since 1998. Buta Jewels has designed classic highest standards of quality jewelry with the latest taste in time.

Our designs that are graceful and stylish, offering an assortment of rings that range from delicate and subtle to intricate and sophisticated.
